About us

What are Animaliens?

We come from the planet Lien and our world is inspired by the number you call 8. To us, we see infinity ♾ which is the preservation of life. Our goal is to build a conscious community that inspires others to not just want to change the world, but truly work towards improving it.

FAQs

Looking for answers?

Why are you developing your own marketplace when others already exist like jpg.store or cnft.io?

Our marketplace was originally built to host our unique smart contract but we have iterated so it will have multiple functionalities besides being a place to trade your NFTs with 0% transaction fees. Our marketplace will also be a store where you can spend your $IEN to purchase a variety of things that the community votes on. For example, tickets and bidding for blue chip NFTs, tickets for tuition/debt raffles, buying education/self-development related products.

How will you be transparent with the community for your philanthrophic endeavours?

After selling out, we will take 8% of the drop and divide them with our partner institutions for donation. We will showcase all transactions for the community to see.

How are royalties going to be distributed with transparency?

Our own smart contract in the Nebula marketplace will allow us to automatically split the 8% royalty fee into 3 different wallets. These will be on the site for the community to see. 1) 50% to holders 2) 25% to project development 3) 25% to the team.

What happens when our Animalien NFTs are not trading in the Nebula marketplace but in jpg.store for example?

On the 8th of each month, we will showcase the total royalties we recieve from all marketplaces and distribute the funds to the appropriate wallets displayed on the site with full transparency.

But how and where do I get $IEN from?

That is another functionality of our marketplace. In Phase 2, you will be able to stake your NFTs in order to earn $IEN.

What if I'm not a student or don't have student debt and win the tuition lottery?

The choice is yours! You can gift it to a family member, a friend, or sponsor one of our mentees for their tuition. You can also use it for your own educational development e.g., coding bootcamp. If you prefer to cash out and take fiat you will incur an 18% fee that will be utilized by community vote.

Will you have a rarity table?

Yes, absolutely! This will be available on cnft.tools shortly after mint.

What inspiration is behind the art?
